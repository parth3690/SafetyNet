# Privacy Policy Updates - Industry Alignment
## Comparison with Google, Facebook, and Snapchat Practices

**Date:** November 14, 2025

## Executive Summary

The Circle Privacy Policy has been updated to align with industry-standard data collection and sharing practices used by major technology companies like Google, Facebook (Meta), and Snapchat, while maintaining compliance with GDPR and CCPA and preserving our core safety-focused mission.

**Key Changes:**
- ✅ Expanded data collection (advertising IDs, device info, usage analytics)
- ✅ Added advertising and marketing data sharing disclosures
- ✅ Extended data retention periods for business purposes
- ✅ Included cookies and tracking technologies section
- ✅ Clarified CCPA "sale" of data and opt-out mechanisms
- ✅ Enhanced third-party service provider disclosures

---

## Comparison with Industry Leaders

### 1. Data Collection Practices

#### **Google (Google Maps, Location Services)**
- ✅ Collects: Location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, advertising IDs
- ✅ Uses: Service delivery, personalization, advertising, analytics
- ✅ Retains: Location history (user-controlled), analytics data (up to 26 months)

#### **Facebook/Meta**
- ✅ Collects: Location data, device info, usage patterns, advertising IDs, social connections
- ✅ Uses: Service delivery, advertising, personalization, analytics
- ✅ Retains: Data while account active, analytics data (up to 2 years)

#### **Snapchat**
- ✅ Collects: Location (Snap Map), device identifiers, usage data, advertising IDs
- ✅ Uses: Service features, advertising, analytics, personalization
- ✅ Retains: Location data (user-controlled), analytics (up to 2 years)

#### **Circle (Updated)**
- ✅ Collects: Location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, advertising IDs (with consent)
- ✅ Uses: Safety monitoring (primary), service improvement, advertising (opt-in), analytics
- ✅ Retains: Location (30 days active), analytics (3 years anonymized), advertising (per partner policies)
- ✅ **Differentiator**: Safety-first focus, minimal data collection for core service

**Alignment Status:** ✅ **ALIGNED** - Similar data collection practices, with safety-focused differentiation

### 2. Advertising & Data Sharing

#### **Industry Standard (Google, Facebook, Snapchat)**
- Share advertising IDs with ad networks
- Use data for personalized advertising
- Share aggregated data with partners
- Retargeting and attribution tracking
- CCPA "sale" disclosures (sharing for advertising)

#### **Circle (Updated)**
- ✅ Share advertising IDs (with opt-in consent)
- ✅ Personalized advertising (opt-in)
- ✅ Attribution and measurement
- ✅ CCPA opt-out mechanism ("Do Not Sell")
- ✅ Clear disclosure of advertising practices

**Alignment Status:** ✅ **ALIGNED** - Matches industry practices with enhanced opt-in/opt-out controls

### 4. Data Retention

#### **Industry Standard**
- Location data: User-controlled or 30-90 days
- Analytics: 2-3 years (anonymized)
- Account data: While active + 2-3 years after deletion
- Advertising data: Per partner policies (typically 1-2 years)

#### **Circle (Updated)**
- ✅ Location: 30 days active, 1 year emergencies, 3 years anonymized
- ✅ Analytics: 3 years (anonymized)
- ✅ Account: While active, 2 years after deletion
- ✅ Advertising: Per partner policies

**Alignment Status:** ✅ **ALIGNED** - Similar retention periods with safety-focused exceptions

## What Stays Different (Our Safety Focus)

While we've aligned with industry practices, Circle maintains key differentiators:

1. **Safety-First Mission**: Core service is safety monitoring, not advertising
2. **Minimal Core Collection**: Essential safety data only, advertising is opt-in
3. **Circle Privacy**: Routine patterns never shared, even with circle members
4. **Emergency Focus**: Vital interests legal basis for emergency data use
5. **User Control**: Enhanced privacy controls and opt-out mechanisms
