## Compliance Gaps & Recommendations

### üî¥ Critical Issues
**None identified** - Policy is compliant with GDPR and CCPA requirements.

### üü° Minor Recommendations

1. **Consent Logging** (GDPR Article 7)
   - **Recommendation**: Implement system to log consent timestamps and methods
   - **Priority**: Medium
   - **Timeline**: 3 months

2. **"Do Not Sell" Link** (CCPA)
   - **Recommendation**: Add "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link to website footer (even if no sale occurs, for transparency)
   - **Priority**: Low
   - **Timeline**: 1 month

3. **Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA)** (GDPR Article 35)
   - **Recommendation**: Conduct Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) for high-risk processing (location tracking, AI processing)
   - **Priority**: High
   - **Timeline**: 6 months

4. **Technical Documentation** (GDPR Article 30)
   - **Recommendation**: Maintain Records of Processing Activities (ROPA) document
   - **Priority**: High
   - **Timeline**: 3 months

5. **Cookie Policy** (if applicable)
   - **Recommendation**: If website uses cookies, add separate Cookie Policy
   - **Priority**: Low
   - **Timeline**: As needed
